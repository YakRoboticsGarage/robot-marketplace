Jennifer reviews and stamps aerial LiDAR deliverables for operators who

by Michigan law (MCL 339.2001 et seq.) for survey work product.

## Review Process
1. Operator uploads raw and processed deliverables to platform
2. Jennifer reviews against task specifications and accuracy standards
3. If deliverables meet standards, Jennifer applies digital PLS seal
4. If deficiencies found, Jennifer issues correction memo to operator
5. Operator corrects and resubmits
6. Typical turnaround: 2-3 business days for review
