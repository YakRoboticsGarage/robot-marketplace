## Review Process
1. Operator uploads registered point cloud and processed deliverables
2. David reviews scan registration, point spacing, and coverage
3. David verifies wall thickness measurements and structural condition documentation
4. If deliverables meet standards, David applies digital PLS seal
5. If deficiencies found, David issues correction memo with specific re-scan requirements
6. Typical turnaround: 3-5 business days (tunnel data is complex)
