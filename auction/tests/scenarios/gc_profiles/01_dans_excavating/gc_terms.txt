DAN'S EXCAVATING INC.
STANDARD SUBCONTRACT TERMS — SURVEY SERVICES

1. INDEMNIFICATION
Subcontractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Contractor from claims
arising from Subcontractor's negligence or willful misconduct.
(Intermediate form per MCL 691.991)

2. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Total liability shall not exceed 1.5x the subcontract value.

3. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
- CGL: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
- Professional E&O: $1,000,000 per claim / $2,000,000 aggregate
- Aviation liability: $5,000,000 (if applicable)
- Workers Compensation: statutory limits
- Additional insured: Dan's Excavating Inc.

4. PAYMENT TERMS
Net 30 from invoice approval. Progress billing monthly.

5. RETAINAGE
10% retained until final acceptance of all deliverables.

6. DATA OWNERSHIP
All deliverables become property of Contractor upon payment.
Subcontractor retains proprietary methods and software.

7. STANDARD OF CARE
Professional standard of care for licensed surveyors in Michigan.

8. CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES
Mutual waiver of consequential, incidental, and indirect damages.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Step 1: Direct negotiation (14 calendar days)
Step 2: Mediation per AAA Construction Industry Rules (30 days)
Step 3: Binding arbitration per AAA Construction Industry Rules

10. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 14 calendar days written notice.
Payment for work completed and accepted prior to termination.

11. CHANGE ORDERS
Written approval required. Equitable adjustment to price and schedule.

12. PLS RESPONSIBILITY
Subcontractor shall provide PLS supervision and stamp all deliverables.
