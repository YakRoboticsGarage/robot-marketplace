AJAX PAVING INDUSTRIES, INC.
STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS FOR SURVEY SUBCONTRACTORS

Effective: January 1, 2026
Form: AJX-SURV-2026-01 (Intermediate)

1. SCOPE AND COMPENSATION
   1.1. Subcontractor shall perform survey services as described in the
        Purchase Order / Task Authorization issued under these terms.
   1.2. Compensation is lump sum unless specified as T&M with a not-to-exceed cap.
   1.3. Payment terms: Net 30 from approved invoice date.
   1.4. Retainage: 5% withheld until final deliverable acceptance.

2. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
   2.1. Commercial General Liability: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
   2.2. Professional Liability (E&O): $1,000,000 per claim / $2,000,000 aggregate
   2.3. Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit
   2.4. Workers' Compensation: Statutory limits
   2.5. Unmanned Aircraft (if applicable): $1,000,000 per occurrence
   2.6. Ajax Paving must be named as Additional Insured on CGL policy.
   2.7. Certificates of Insurance due prior to mobilization.

3. PROFESSIONAL STANDARDS
   3.1. All survey work shall be performed under the supervision of a
        Professional Land Surveyor licensed in the State of Michigan.
   3.2. Subcontractor shall comply with all applicable MDOT standards
        and specifications referenced in the task authorization.
   3.3. Data shall be delivered in coordinate systems and formats specified
        in the scope of work.

4. SCHEDULE AND DELAYS
   4.1. Time is of the essence.
   4.2. Subcontractor shall commence within 5 calendar days of NTP.
   4.3. Liquidated damages: $500/calendar day for late completion.
   4.4. Force majeure: Weather days exceeding 3 consecutive days of
        precipitation extend schedule day-for-day.

5. DELIVERABLE REVIEW AND ACCEPTANCE
   5.1. Ajax shall review deliverables within 10 business days of submission.
   5.2. Subcontractor shall correct deficiencies within 5 business days
        of written notice at no additional cost.
   5.3. Final acceptance constitutes approval for payment of retainage.

6. SAFETY AND SITE ACCESS
   6.1. Subcontractor shall comply with Ajax safety program and MDOT
        work zone safety requirements.
   6.2. All field personnel must complete Ajax site orientation.
   6.3. High-visibility apparel required at all times on active roadway.
   6.4. Maintenance of Traffic (MOT) plans subject to Ajax approval.

7. INDEMNIFICATION
   7.1. Subcontractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Ajax Paving,
        its officers, employees, and agents from claims arising from
        Subcontractor's negligent acts or omissions.

8. OWNERSHIP OF WORK PRODUCT
   8.1. All deliverables become property of Ajax Paving upon payment.
   8.2. Ajax grants Subcontractor right to retain copies for professional
        records and portfolio use.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   9.1. Disputes shall first be addressed through direct negotiation
        between project managers (14 calendar days).
   9.2. Unresolved disputes escalate to mediation under AAA rules.
   9.3. Venue: Oakland County, Michigan.

10. CONFIDENTIALITY
    10.1. Subcontractor shall not disclose project-specific data, pricing,
          or Ajax proprietary information without written consent.

11. TERMINATION
    11.1. Ajax may terminate for convenience with 7 days written notice.
    11.2. Subcontractor compensated for work completed to termination date.

12. COMPLIANCE
    12.1. Subcontractor shall comply with all federal, state, and local laws.
    12.2. DBE/SBE participation goals apply if specified in task authorization.
    12.3. E-Verify compliance required.
