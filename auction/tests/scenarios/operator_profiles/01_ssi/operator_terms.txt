SURVEYING SOLUTIONS INC.
STANDARD OPERATOR TERMS — ROBOT TASK MARKETPLACE

Effective: January 1, 2026

These terms govern SSI's participation as an operator on the robot task
marketplace. They cover 12 dimensions of the operator-platform relationship.

1. SERVICE COMMITMENT
   SSI commits to completing accepted tasks within the agreed timeline.
   Mobilization within 48 hours of task acceptance for standard tasks.
   Emergency mobilization (24 hours) available at 1.5x standard rate.

2. QUALITY STANDARDS
   All deliverables meet or exceed accuracy specifications in the task order.
   Independent QC check performed before submission.
   PLS review and stamp on all survey deliverables per Michigan law.

3. EQUIPMENT STANDARDS
   All equipment calibrated and maintained per manufacturer specifications.
   Backup equipment available for critical missions.
   Equipment list and calibration records provided on request.

4. INSURANCE MAINTENANCE
   SSI maintains insurance coverage meeting or exceeding platform minimums.
   Updated COI provided within 5 business days of policy renewal.
   Platform notified within 24 hours of any coverage lapse or cancellation.

5. LICENSING AND CERTIFICATION
   All applicable licenses and certifications kept current.
   Platform notified 60 days before any license expiration.
   Recertification documentation uploaded within 5 days of renewal.

6. DATA HANDLING
   All project data transmitted via encrypted channels.
   Raw data retained for 3 years per Michigan retention requirements.
   Client data deleted from field equipment within 30 days of delivery.

7. SAFETY PROTOCOLS
   OSHA compliance on all field operations.
   Site-specific safety plans for confined space, traffic, aerial work.
   Incident reporting within 4 hours of any safety event.

8. COMMUNICATION
   Task status updates at minimum daily during active field work.
   Response to platform/client inquiries within 4 business hours.
   Final deliverable status notification within 2 hours of upload.

9. PRICING AND PAYMENT
   Bid prices are firm for 30 days from submission.
   Payment accepted via platform escrow (ACH or Stripe).
   No additional charges without written task order amendment.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
    Good-faith direct negotiation (14 days).
    Platform mediation service if direct negotiation fails.
    Binding arbitration per AAA rules as last resort.

11. PLATFORM CONDUCT
    Accurate representation of qualifications and experience.
    No bid manipulation, shill bidding, or market gaming.
    Compliance with platform terms of service and code of conduct.

12. SUBCONTRACTING
    SSI may use approved subcontractors with platform notification.
    SSI remains responsible for all deliverables and quality.
    Subcontractor compliance documents uploaded before field deployment.
