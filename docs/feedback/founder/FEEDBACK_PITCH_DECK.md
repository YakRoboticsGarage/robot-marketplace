# Feedback: Pitch Deck Issues

**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Source:** Founder review

## Critical Issues

1. **Missing sources / evidence** — Numbers are stated without backing. "368K Part 107 holders" — says who? "$8B TAM" — from where? Every claim needs a source or it's a red flag.

2. **"0 competitors" is a red flag** — Investors hear "nobody's doing this" as either "you haven't looked" or "there's no market." Reframe: there ARE adjacent players, we have a differentiated position. Show a 2x2 or competitive positioning matrix.

3. **Vision vs reality is blurred** — The deck mixes what's built (auction engine) with what's a demo (yakrobot.bid) with what's a plan (Michigan pilot) with what's aspirational (lunar). An investor can't tell what's real. Need clear "built / in progress / planned / vision" labels.

4. **Vanity metrics** — "147 tests" and "15 MCP tools" mean nothing to an investor. They care about: customers, revenue, LOIs, waitlist, engagement. We have none of those yet. Better to be honest about stage than dress up dev metrics.

5. **Timelines are misleading** — "v4.0 (2028+): Lunar" in 4 years is not credible and undermines the rest. Use phase gates instead of years. "Phase 4: When construction + mining are proven and NASA commercial programs mature."

6. **Missing 2x2 framing** — The competitive slide lists companies but doesn't show positioning. A 2x2 (e.g., "Automation level" x "Physical world execution") would instantly show the white space we occupy.

7. **No customer voice** — No quotes, no pain data from real conversations (we haven't had them yet). At minimum, reference the real MDOT RFQ and the real GC landscape research.
