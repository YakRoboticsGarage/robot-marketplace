# Michigan Survey Standards Reference

Load this file when processing Michigan (MDOT, county, or municipal) RFPs.

## MDOT Accuracy Standards (95% confidence)

| Control Type | Horizontal | Vertical |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Control | ±0.05 ft | ±0.05 ft closure |
| Intermediate Control | ±0.07 ft avg; ±0.10 ft max | ±0.10 ft accumulated |
| Design Survey Hard Surfaces | ±0.05 ft | ±0.01 ft; ±0.02 ft relative |
| Topographic Ground | N/A | ±0.10 ft accumulated |

## MDOT Cross-Section Requirements (Section 104.09)

- Cross-sections every 50 feet for quantity determination
- Hard surfaces to nearest 0.01 foot
- Ground to nearest 0.1 foot
- Horizontal control points max 1000 feet apart on tangent
- Bench marks every 1000 feet

## MDOT LiDAR Standards (Chapter 9)

### Static Terrestrial Laser Scanning (STLS)
- Point density: max 0.1 ft spacing on structures, max 0.3 ft on roads
- Minimum 4 control points per project
- Minimum 20% scan overlap
- Minimum 20 hard surface check observations
- Validation within 0.05 ft of adjusted point cloud
- Deliverables: e57, LAS, or LAZ format
- NSSDA-formatted accuracy reports required

### Mobile Terrestrial LiDAR (MTL)
- Must meet NCHRP Report 748 Category 1A for design projects
- Acquisition on dry pavement at traffic speeds
- RGB colorization images required along trajectory

## MDOT Survey Types (Chapter 4)

1. Control Survey — primary/intermediate control
2. Alignment/Right of Way Survey — legal boundaries
3. Design Survey (Topographic) — surface features for design
4. Bridge/Structure Survey — structure location and integration
5. Hydraulic Survey — bridge/culvert design data
6. Certified Survey — real estate acquisition
7. Photogrammetric Control Survey — ground control for aerial

## MDOT Contractor Staking (Section 824)

- Annual third-party equipment calibration required
- Licensed professional surveyor oversight
- Quality Control Plan submission required
- Control verification before construction start

## Coordinate Systems

- Horizontal: Michigan State Plane, South Zone (most projects) or Central/North
- Datum: NAD83(2011) horizontal, NAVD88 vertical
- For Great Lakes projects: IGLD85 vertical datum
